# Contact and Platforms

Use this skill when a request needs official public Ghost Orgy links or outreach details.

## Primary sources

- `/press/` is the canonical page for public contact and platform links.
- `/` may surface release links and listening entry points.

## Official routes to check

- contact email
- SoundCloud
- Spotify
- Apple Music
- Amazon Music
- Instagram
- TikTok
- Facebook
- Pinterest

## Response guidance

- Prefer the exact public URLs listed on the site.
- If multiple pages mention the same platform, use the `/press/` version as the tie-breaker.
- State clearly when a link is a streaming profile versus a social profile.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent private booking contacts or unpublished platform accounts.
- Keep summaries aligned with the public site and press wording.
