Lore

The tracks leak out of a larger canon: the Orchard, the Watchers, the Nine Sisters, and the reports left after contact.

The Nine Sisters were human once. Hell took them, changed them, and lost custody. They move now through the Orchard of Unmaking, where devotion rots into weather and every path behaves like a decision already made.

Each sister arrives with a damage pattern. Some alter rooms. Some alter memory. Some only need a voice and a witness. What remains afterward is physical: rewritten text, impact symmetry, impossible time, faces that keep changing between copies.

The Nine Sisters

Human once. Taken by Hell. Cast out when Hell couldn't hold them. Each one governs a domain. Each one leaves evidence.

I
Annihilation of anchor

Limbo

Motion without landing. Unsealed eyes that never close. She breaks clocks, loops corridors, and removes the feeling that a present moment can hold still. Witnesses report delayed sound, vestibular collapse, and a woman in grey who appears only in reflections.

Trace · clock stutter / grey habit / wind like sideways breath
II
Desire that overwrites self

Lust

Consumptive wanting. She dissolves identity into pursuit until the subject becomes pure desire with no one left inside it. Thermal blooms independent of temperature. Violet condensation on mirrors. A voice described as slow, coiling, and impossible to stop listening to.

Trace · ozone scent / bruise-purple shadow / pulse as pursuit
III
Starvation of meaning

Gluttony

Not hunger for food. Hunger for significance. She drains color, joy, and purpose from achievement until the ache itself is all that remains. Subjects inhale the essence of their awards. Objects turn grey. A pit-shaped shadow opens in the floor.

Trace · thin gravity / oxygen-starved air / the pit that never fills
IV
Value extraction

Greed

She audits souls like a cosmic ledger. Worthless objects marked as millions. Real gold marked as decoy. Officers at the scene develop compulsive hoarding behavior from proximity alone. Copper taste. Involuntary hand-clenching. Hunger becomes the only appreciating asset.

Trace · copper mouth / clutching hands / hollowed worth
V
Judgment as fire

Wrath

Selective thermal execution. Victims burn from the inside while bedding stays cold. Ash falls from nothing. A layered female voice speaks through the bodies of those nearby. Hands measured at 600 degrees. Reaction time of 0.4 seconds — faster than human perception allows.

Trace · internal heat / ash without source / scorched voice
VI
Counter-doctrine

Heresy

Stitched mouth. One eye socket holds a contained ember; the other is empty. Charred black habit. She does not argue with faith — she makes it forget its own words. Bells ring silently. Scripture reorders on the page. Prayer becomes fuel for the manifestation instead of defense against it.

Trace · silent bells / burning socket / rewritten text
VII
Structural resonance

Violence

Harm that mirrors itself across bodies and rooms. Victim's skeleton fractures match the cracks in the walls. Symmetrical breaks across every bone. Phantom healing — tissue behaves as though the wound happened years ago. Photographs show wood grain where marrow should be.

Trace · mirrored fractures / bone as architecture / pressure in the ribs
VIII
Identity replacement

Fraud

Porcelain mask over frantic eyes. She wears the victim's face and walks like she lives there. Caller IDs shift mid-sentence. Memories invert. Two voices speak in unison. A sound like a mirror shattering, sustained for twelve seconds. When it stops, only one identity remains.

Trace · stolen face / sustained shatter / edited memory
IX
Weaponized trust

Treachery

Stitched-flesh face. Broken rings. She rewires perception so that love reads as threat. Smart home recordings show the victim saying "rest and be free" — the subject heard "confess and bleed." A 19-hertz hum. Forty-degree temperature drop. Frost on the weapon in a tropical climate.

Trace · inverted meaning / frost where there shouldn't be / 19 Hz hum
