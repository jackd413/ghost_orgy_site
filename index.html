Lore

The project lives between experimental audio, horror fiction, and obsessive production. The Nine Sisters and the Orchard of Unmaking thread through everything.

Ghost Orgy is not a touring act or a live band. It is a canon, a ritual system, and a set of haunted rules that keep showing up in sound, text, and image.

Each piece is treated as an encounter. Close-mic whispers that feel too near. Detail rich atmospheres. Low end that does not stay in the speakers. Structures that follow the Sisters more than safe song templates.

At the core are the Nine Sisters human in origin, taken by Hell, then expelled when they destabilized its structure. They move through the Orchard of Unmaking and pull people in through obsession, hunger, wrong motion, and fracture.

Operating constraints

The work has a few hard rules. If a track does not hit these, it does not belong here.

  • No safe background music.
  • High density, lossless friendly sound design.
  • Narratives that reward repeat listening.
  • Canon coherence across tracks, art, and text.

The Nine Sisters

Expelled archetypes. Each with her own law, voice, and ritual. Together they mark the cracks Heaven tried to name out of existence.

Circle I • Limbo
Limbo
Annihilation of anchor
Acceleration without arrival. Pace without center. She breaks clocks, sequences, and the idea that a present moment can ever hold still.
Circle II • Lust
Lust
Desire that overwrites self
Obsession turned cannibal. She amplifies want until memory and motive collapse and only the object remains.
Circle III • Gluttony
Gluttony
Starvation of meaning
Not hunger for food. Hunger for significance. She drains color, joy, and purpose until only the ache and the need to fill it are left.
Circle IV • Greed
Greed
Wealth as disease
Gold fused to flesh. Every grasping instinct ritualized. Thrones built out of other people and called success.
Circle V • Wrath
Wrath
Force without repair
Impact as language. She speaks in breaks, shatters, and blows that all say the same thing: nothing is going back how it was.
Circle VI • Heresy
Heresy
Speech against structure
She talks in loopholes and misalignments. Doctrine melts around her. Systems forget how to hold their own weight.
Circle VII • Violence
Violence
Cruelty as grammar
Harm repeated until it becomes structure. Wounds multiplied in stone, glass, and mirrored surfaces that never quite stop reflecting.
Circle VIII • Fraud
Fraud
False faces
Porcelain reflections and mirrored eyes. She does not argue with truth. She replaces it with whatever performs better in the moment.
Circle IX • Treachery
Treachery
Betrayal as architecture
Mechanical stillness. Leathery husk mask. The quiet realization that the floor under you was traded away a long time ago.

Music

Built for headphones and systems that can move air. Detail forward, effect heavy, but not polished into something safe.

Ghost Orgy on SoundCloud - latest ritual cuts

The signal chain is set up to keep texture intact instead of sanding it down. Close dynamic vocals, heavy modulation, and enough headroom for the quiet parts to stay dangerous.

The term die-fi is a working rule: high production value and lossless detail kept in service of something damaged and uncomfortable instead of comforting.
