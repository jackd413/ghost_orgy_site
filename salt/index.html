Salt

Salt is the debut album from Ghost Orgy, a Phoenix-based experimental post-hardcore project by Jack Dyer. Art-damaged hardcore, literary heavy music, die-fi ritual audio, and a horror-mythic world built to hit the body before the mind catches up.
